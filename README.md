# Embryo Predation and Parental Care on *Nocomis* Nests
## *Mr. Mom:* Nocomis *embryo-burying effectively increases embryo survival amidst predation by all mutualism participants*

The following repository contains all data, codes, and information related to a study conducted by the Frimpong Lab at Virginia Tech, which investigated the role of *Nocomis* parental care (through the act of embryo burying) and how the threat of predation changes across the landscape of *Nocomis* nests. Additionally, this study investigated  now published in *Ecology of Freshwater Fish*.
